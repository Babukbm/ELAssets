Many dinner parties are planned during the holiday season, so why not dress up your dinner table with your own homemade holiday themed place-mats? The possible creations are endless and can be custom designed to fit your party theme. Dress up your holiday table this year with a fun activity the whole family can partake in.

There are so many possibilities that the only thing you have to have is clear contact paper, the rest of the items will be up to you, but below is a list of items just to get you started:

  • Poster Board cut to placemat size
  • Crayons, makers, paint, stickers
  • Leaves, pine needles, dried flowers
  • Appliqué and fabric scraps
  • Can of fake snow
  • YOUR IMAGINATION!
  1. First determine what shape you want your placemats to be: square, round, star-shaped, etc.
  2. Cut 2 pieces of contact paper about ¼" larger than the intended placemat size.
  3. Come up with a design and then use whatever creative elements you want to layout your pattern on one piece of the contact paper using the sticky side up to hold the pattern in place.
  4. Once your design is finished, place the second piece of contact paper sticky side down to seal the surface.
  5. Trim the edges with scissors so that you have a perfect edge.
  6. Place your homemade Holiday Placemats on your dinner table for a wonderful addition to your place setting.
