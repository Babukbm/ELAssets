Thanksgiving is such a wonderful holiday for sharing a meal with your family. Make your table extra special this year with these delightful “Turkey Napkins”. Your guests will be charmed and your kids will love making them.

  1. Print out the Turkey Napkin pattern and trace onto colorful posterboard or construction paper. Cut out pattern.
  2. Use a craft knife to cut 2 wings near the center of the band.
  3. Tape a few craft feathers together and then tape them to the inside of the wings.
  4. Curl the cardboard by wrapping the ends of the band around something round, such as a rolling pin. Remove the band, overlap the ends, and secure them with double-sided tape.
  5. Use a glue stick to attach 2 googly eyes, a red paper wattle, and a yellow paper beak.
  6. To give your turkey a fan of feathers, simply fold a colorful napkin accordion style and tuck it inside the ring. The turkey can be used with cloth or paper napkins.
